I create totally free music for creators

The current state of music licensing and monitization is abysmal and quite frankly, unsustainable. I’m cutting all out the crap,creating the best music I can, and giving it away for anyone to use for any purpose.

Got a podcast or YouTube channel? Livestreaming D&D? Making screencasts or wedding videos? Go ahead, use my music. I made it for folks just for you. If you choose to support me and my efforts on Patreon, you will get access to bonus tracks every month.
